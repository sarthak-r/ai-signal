APRA CPS 230 bites tomorrow: AI-vendor contracts must comply by 1 JulyAustralia · regulatory

Under APRA's CPS 230 (Operational Risk Management), regulated entities must have material service-provider arrangements — including AI/LLM vendor contracts — brought into compliance by the next renewal and no later than 1 July 2026. Existing AFS-licensee obligations (best-interests duty, accountability for outputs) apply with full force whether advice is generated by a human or an AI tool.

If you sell into or operate within AU financial services, your model/API vendor terms and your own customer contracts need CPS 230 operational-risk and continuity clauses now — this is a tomorrow deadline, not a 2026 theme. Check your Anthropic/OpenAI/Google terms against it before relying on them in a regulated workflow.
