The builders push back on agent autonomy: keep humans in the loop, route smarter, test before you ship

The app layer is where today's signal lives. The loudest voices — Jon Udell, Simon Willison, Sebastian Raschka, Nathan Lambert — are converging on a thesis: stop stacking agents on agents, and instead give humans tighter control loops, local execution, and real eval harnesses. The open-model field is splintering into specialized players you should pick by license and architecture, not brand. For a regulated wealthtech shop, the through-line is auditability: local-first knowledge bases, per-prompt routing, and pass@k testing are now off-the-shelf.
